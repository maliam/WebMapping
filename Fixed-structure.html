How do I define 'Home'?

by Malia Mulligan

In this project I explore the concepts of 'home' and 'hometown' and describe the three different places I call home

Fox Island, Washington

Fox Island, Washington was the place I grew up. I wasn't born in Washington, but my family moved there in 2001 when I wasn't even one. My family lived there for 17 years.

Kailua, Hawaii

I moved to Kailua, Hawaii with my parents when I was 16 years old. While I only lived there for 2 years while I was in highschool, this place has a lot of meaning to my family. My mom grew up on the island of Oahu, as did my maternal grandparents. Furthermore, Oahu was the place that my parents met. Plus, thats the place that my parents and grandma still live, and the place that I go home to for holidays.

Eugene, Oregon

Eugene, Oregon is where I've attended college for the last four years. Eugene is the place that I lived on my own for the first time, the place that I rented my own home for the first time, and the place that I feel like I've really come into my own.
